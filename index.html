Biography

Luke is from Vancouver Washington and has been working on his craft for the last six years through a variety of projects and classes. Luke’s primary area of study is Meinser technique as he believes being truthful and present in the imaginary circumstances of film is the key to any story.

News

Luke is currently working on a Docu-style comedy written and directed by Tommy Harrington titled Dick Strange American Made. The show follows and underachieving adult film director who encounters the one thing that could forever change his ways: A nine-year old daughter he never knew he had. Luke's character Jimmy Nails, is one of of D.S's main male actors.

Luke is currently filming the much anticipated horror film Get Gone, a story of a group of hikers who stumble across the wrong group of mountain folk. Luke will be playing Scott Whizzer, acting alongside Lin Shaye and Weston Cage.

Luke just recently wrapped on a very exciting project for FLIR Camera Systems produced by R-West production studios. Luke had the opportunity to portray a rescue diver as he repelled down a harness into the "ocean" to save a person in distress.
